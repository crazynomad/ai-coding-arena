## Prompt

Create an interactive web animation that tells the story of Baron Munchausen pulling himself (and his horse) out of a swamp by his own hair.

The animation should play as a sequence of scenes:
1. Baron walking confidently, then suddenly sinking into swamp
2. Panic! Struggling, sinking deeper
3. The "eureka" moment
4. Grabs own hair and starts pulling UP
5. Slowly, impossibly, he rises out of the mud
6. Triumphant pose on solid ground

Requirements:
- Pure HTML/CSS/JS, no frameworks, no build step
- Canvas-based animation, 60fps target
- Strong art style and atmosphere
- Expressive character with personality
- Comic timing and humor
- Auto-plays on load, click to advance/replay
- Particle effects (mud, bubbles, sparkles)
